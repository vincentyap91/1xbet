Esports Cashback Boom

Place bets on esports and get up to 20% cashback weekly!

How to take part in this bonus offer

  1. Register or log in to your 1xBet account.
  2. Click Take part on this page to join the promotion.
  3. Place bets of at least 21 MYR on esports with odds of 1.50 or higher (for singles), or accumulator bets with odds of 1.40 or higher per selection.
  4. Level up each week to receive higher cashback on your net losses from qualifying esports bets.

How to reach different levels

Cashback is calculated from the total stake of eligible esports bets placed during each weekly period. Reach a higher level to unlock a higher cashback percentage:

  • Level 1: between 100 MYR and 2,046.2 MYR — 5% cashback
  • Level 2: between 2,047 MYR and 4,092.4 MYR — 10% cashback
  • Level 3: between 4,093 MYR and 10,229.9 MYR — 15% cashback
  • Level 4: 10,230 MYR and above — 20% cashback

Maximum weekly cashback: 26,499 MYR. Cashback is credited as bonus funds to your account.
